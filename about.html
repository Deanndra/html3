What made me wanna build this website was when I was younger I had trouble talking to people face to face and I still do kind of. When gaming was introduced to me I was meeting all types of people who were either different or the same like me and it made me so happy that I could do something that I am really interested in while also building connections. So I decided to at least persuade others to try gaming by building a website dedicated to help them get started.

This website is intended for beginner gamers of all people no matter who they are. I want people to at least experience the world of gaming like I did by joining communities, meeting new people, and gaining a new interest/hobby. I hope my website can help people who are new to gaming and get them started on this path.
